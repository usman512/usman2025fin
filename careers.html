Why work with J&J Fundforce

J&J Fundforce is a dynamic door-to-door fundraising company based in Ottawa, Ontario. We specialize in connecting communities with meaningful causes, providing professional fundraising services that drive results. Our passionate team is dedicated to making a positive impact, one doorstep at a time.

At J&J Fundforce, we don’t just knock on doors—we level up every day. When we’re not out raising money for amazing causes, you’ll find us in the office battling it out on Xbox. With two consoles set up, we play all the sports games—NHL, NBA 2K, Madden, FIFA, UFC, MLB The Show—you name it, we play it. We work hard, play harder, and keep the vibes high all day long. Fundraising with us isn’t just a job—it’s a lifestyle.
